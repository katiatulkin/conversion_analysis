# Analysis Summary & Insights

## Funnel Analysis

A significant drop-off occurs between the cart and conversion stages.

While a large proportion of users add items to their cart, only a small percentage complete the purchase, suggesting friction during the checkout process.

**Recommendation:**
- Simplify checkout flow
- Reduce steps required to purchase
- Introduce trust signals (e.g. delivery info, payment security)

## Channel & Device Performance

Desktop and social channels show the highest conversion rates, while mobile organic traffic drives the largest volume but underperforms in conversion.

This indicates that while mobile organic is effective for acquisition, it presents the greatest opportunity for optimisation.

**Recommendation:**
- Improve mobile UX and page load speed
- Optimise landing pages for organic traffic
- Test mobile-specific conversion flows

## Behaviour Analysis

Converting users exhibit significantly higher engagement, including longer session durations, more page views, and substantially higher cart activity.

Cart additions are the strongest indicator of purchase intent.

**Recommendation:**
- Encourage cart interactions through prompts and recommendations
- Improve product discovery and navigation
- Use urgency tactics (e.g. low stock, limited-time offers)

## Customer Value

Customers who both use the app and opt into email consistently generate the highest lifetime value across all segments.

This suggests that multi-channel engagement plays a key role in driving long-term revenue.

**Recommendation:**
- Promote app downloads through incentives
- Increase email sign-up rates during onboarding
- Personalise communication for engaged users

## Product Insights

Higher-rated products generate greater revenue per product, with 'Excellent'-rated items outperforming lower-rated categories.

However, mid-tier products contribute the most total revenue due to their higher volume.

Improving product ratings presents a clear revenue opportunity.

**Recommendation:**
- Encourage customer reviews post-purchase
- Improve product quality and descriptions
- Highlight high-rated products more prominently
